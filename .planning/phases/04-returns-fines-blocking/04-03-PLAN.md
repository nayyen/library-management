## Phase Goal

**As a** mahasiswa, **I want to** see my fines, overdue indicators, and why my account is blocked, **so that** I understand what I owe and can settle it to borrow again.

<objective>
Deliver the mahasiswa-visibility vertical slice end-to-end, completing the "visible to both the mahasiswa and pustakawan" half of RET-02: the backend returns `denda_tertunggak` (the mahasiswa's summed outstanding denda) on the mahasiswa GET branch, and "Pinjaman Saya" gains a "Denda" column, a "Terlambat" badge on overdue active loans, and a BlockedBanner whose copy is personalized with the actual denda amount.

After Task 3 a blocked or fined mahasiswa can actually see their denda, overdue status, and a personalized block reason — a capability that did not exist after Plan 02.

Purpose: Completes RET-02 (fine visible to the mahasiswa). Implements locked decisions D-07, D-08, D-09.
Output: `denda_tertunggak` in the mahasiswa response, BlockedBanner amount interpolation, Denda column + Terlambat badge + skeleton update on the mahasiswa table, RED→GREEN backend test.

Depends on Plans 01 and 02: reuses the `_is_terlambat`/`is_terlambat` field and `func.sum` aggregate established earlier and extends the same shared files (Wave 3 due to file overlap).
</objective>
